01

Feather Quality Scoring

Our AI camera system continuously analyzes feather condition across the flock, providing automated scoring that would otherwise require hours of manual inspection. Spot deterioration trends early and intervene before welfare or productivity is affected.

  • Automated per-bird feather scoring using computer vision
  • Trend detection over time, revealing deterioration at an early stage
  • Non invasive: camera based, no handling required
  • Real-time dashboard alerts when scores drop below configurable thresholds

02

Poultry Mite Monitoring

Red mites are most active at night, making them notoriously hard to detect before an infestation becomes severe. Our system monitors flock activity patterns during dark hours, identifying restless behavior that indicates mite presence days or weeks before visible signs appear.

  • Night mode camera analysis of flock behavior
  • Early warning alerts based on activity anomalies
  • More accurately plan and execute treatments for maximal effect
  • Keep birds productive and prevent losses from stress and disrupted rest
